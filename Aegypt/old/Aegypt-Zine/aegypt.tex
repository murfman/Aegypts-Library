\chapter{Introduction}
The goal is to list all of the books and authors mentioned throughout Ægypt, or The Solitudes, by chapter. A complete list of those authors are listed at the end in alphabetical order by their first name. If a chapter is missing below, then it did not have any books or authors mentioned within it. Books listed in bold are fictional works created by Crowley. The Authors Mentioned subsections list authors that were mentioned within a chapter but did not mention a particular book.

In most cases I tried to cite a work that would have been appropriate for the timeframe of the story in which it is mentioned. In some cases, I have not been able to find a suitable edition and so cite a modern edition instead. Those will eventually be cleaned up to reflect either a copy of the work from the 16th century, one up to 1978, or one available to Crowley while he was writing.

Future updates Future updates will make use of an actual \textsc{BibTex} file to properly record the books I came across. With this first one, I didn't realize plan things out so much as just jumped in and started making a list. Eventually, I will add in subsequent books in the series, but I’m in no real hurry to do so. This was purely a fun side-project that got my head away from “Real Life” for a time.

I’m open to any ideas as to how this can be updated and expanded. For any corrections or comments the reader can email me directly at murfman@pm.me.

Special thanks goes to the guys within the \#Books channel on \href{https://www.mu-podcast.com}{The Miskatonic University Podcast} \href{https://discord.gg/5nH7UqtpdM}{Discord Server}. You guys know who you are.
